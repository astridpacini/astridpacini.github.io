Calypso: Coherent Lagrangian Pathways from the Surface Ocean to Interio

As a participant in the Calypso project, I study the three dimensional pathways that transport biological and biogeochemical tracers including phytoplankton, nutrients, oxygen, and carbon. At sea, I lead sampling of the biological community structure through optical measurements, flow cytometry, 16S sequencing, and respiration rate measurements.
